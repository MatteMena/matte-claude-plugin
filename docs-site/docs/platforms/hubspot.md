# HubSpot CRM

Connect Claude to your HubSpot account to manage contacts, companies, deals, and tickets using natural language.

## Connecting HubSpot

1. Go to [nerve.mattemena.com](https://nerve.mattemena.com) → **Connected Sources**
2. Click **Connect HubSpot**
3. Authorise access to your HubSpot portal

## What you can do

### Contacts
- List, get, search, create, update, and delete contacts

### Companies
- List, get, search, create, update, and delete companies

### Deals
- List, get, search, create, update, and delete deals

### Tickets
- List, get, search, create, update, and delete support tickets

## Example prompts

```
"Find all HubSpot contacts with a Gmail email address"
"Create a new deal called 'Acme Corp - Q3 Campaign' worth $15,000"
"Update the lifecycle stage of contact john@example.com to 'Customer'"
"List all open tickets created this week"
"Search for companies in the 'Technology' industry"
```

## Use cases

- Cross-reference your HubSpot CRM data with ad platform performance
- Create deals or contacts from ad lead form submissions
- Search your CRM to build custom Meta or TikTok audiences
- Update deal stages based on campaign attribution data
