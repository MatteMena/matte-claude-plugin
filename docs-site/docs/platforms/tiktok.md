# TikTok Ads

Connect Claude to your TikTok for Business account to manage TikTok ad campaigns, ad groups, creatives, audiences, and performance metrics.

## Connecting TikTok

1. Go to [nerve.mattemena.com](https://nerve.mattemena.com) → **Connected Sources**
2. Click **Connect TikTok**
3. Authorise access to your TikTok for Business advertiser accounts

## What you can do

### Accounts & Insights
- List and get advertiser details and summaries
- Pull performance insights with date ranges and breakdowns
- Search terms performance reports

### Campaigns
- List, create, update, change status, and delete campaigns

### Ad Groups
- List, create, update, and delete ad groups
- Configure targeting, placements, budgets

### Ads
- List, create, update, and delete ads
- Manage Spark Ads

### Assets
- List images and videos in your library
- List TikTok identities

### Audiences
- List custom audiences
- Create custom audiences from lists
- Create rule-based audiences
- Create lookalike audiences

### Pixels
- List TikTok pixels
- Get pixel event data

## Example prompts

```
"What are my top performing TikTok ad groups this week?"
"Create a new campaign for my summer sale with a $500 daily budget"
"Show me search term performance for my TikTok campaigns"
"List all TikTok videos in my asset library"
```
