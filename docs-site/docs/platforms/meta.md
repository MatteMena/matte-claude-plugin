# Meta (Facebook / Instagram)

Connect Claude to your Meta Business Manager to manage Facebook and Instagram ad campaigns, creatives, audiences, and pixels using natural language.

## Connecting Meta

1. Go to [nerve.mattemena.com](https://nerve.mattemena.com) → **Connected Sources**
2. Click **Connect Meta**
3. Authorise access to your Meta Business Manager accounts
4. Select the ad accounts you want Claude to access

## What you can do

### Accounts & Insights
- Get your ad account list and summaries
- Pull performance insights with custom date ranges and breakdowns
- Compare campaigns across accounts

### Campaigns
- List, create, update, pause, and delete campaigns
- Set campaign objectives, budgets, and bid strategies

### Ad Sets
- List, create, update, and delete ad sets
- Configure targeting, placements, and schedules

### Ads
- List, create, update, and delete ads
- Preview ads via the Graph API

### Creatives
- List, get, create, and delete ad creatives
- Upload and list ad images and videos

### Audiences
- List custom audiences
- Create custom audiences from customer lists
- Create lookalike audiences

### Pixels
- List Meta pixels
- Get pixel event statistics

## Example prompts

```
"Show me the top 5 campaigns by ROAS last month"
"Pause all ad sets with a CPA above $50"
"Create a lookalike audience based on my top purchasers"
"What's my Facebook spend this week vs last week?"
```
