# Snapchat Ads

Connect Claude to your Snapchat Ads account to manage campaigns, ad squads, creatives, audiences, and performance stats.

## Connecting Snapchat

1. Go to [nerve.mattemena.com](https://nerve.mattemena.com) → **Connected Sources**
2. Click **Connect Snapchat**
3. Authorise access to your Snapchat Business account

## What you can do

- View and manage **campaigns** and **ad squads**
- Create and manage **creatives**
- Manage **audiences** (custom and lookalike)
- Pull **performance stats** with date ranges and breakdowns

## Example prompts

```
"Show me my Snapchat campaign performance this week"
"What's the CPM on my top Snapchat ad squads?"
"List my Snapchat custom audiences"
```
